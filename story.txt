Once upon a hot summer day, a crow was flying in search of water. The sun was blazing, and the crow felt very thirsty. After a long search, it finally spotted a pitcher under a tree.
The crow quickly flew down and looked inside. To its disappointment, there was only a little water at the bottom of the pitcher. The crow tried to reach it, but its beak couldn’t go that deep.
The clever crow thought hard. Suddenly, it noticed some small pebbles lying nearby. An idea struck! The crow picked up the pebbles one by one and dropped them into the pitcher. Slowly, the water level rose higher and higher.
At last, the water reached the top. The crow happily drank the cool water and flew away, refreshed and satisfied.
